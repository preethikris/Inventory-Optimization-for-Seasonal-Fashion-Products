# Inventory Optimization For Seasonal Fashion Products

## Project Overview

 Inventory Optimization For Seasonal Fashion Products is a Business Intelligence solution developed using Power BI to analyze retail operations across sales, inventory, customers, campaigns, and product performance.

The project provides actionable insights that help retail businesses optimize inventory levels, improve customer engagement, evaluate campaign effectiveness, monitor sales trends, and maximize product profitability.

---

## Business Problem

Retail businesses often face challenges such as:

* Inventory stockouts and overstock situations
* Low visibility into customer purchasing behavior
* Ineffective marketing campaigns
* Difficulty identifying profitable products
* Lack of centralized reporting for business decisions

This project addresses these challenges by transforming raw retail data into interactive dashboards and business insights.

---

## Objectives

* Analyze sales performance across products and channels
* Monitor inventory utilization and stock movement
* Understand customer purchasing patterns
* Evaluate campaign effectiveness and discount strategies
* Measure product profitability and inventory ROI
* Track revenue performance against business targets

---

## Dataset Information

The project uses seven interconnected datasets:

### Products

Contains product information such as:

* Product ID
* Product Name
* Category
* Brand
* Color
* Size
* Cost Price
* Catalog Price

### Sales

Contains transaction-level sales data:

* Sale ID
* Customer ID
* Country
* Channel
* Sale Date
* Total Amount

### SalesItems

Contains item-level sales information:

* Product ID
* Quantity Sold
* Item Total
* Unit Price
* Discount Applied

### Customers

Contains customer information:

* Customer ID
* Country
* Age Range
* Signup Date

### Campaigns

Contains promotional campaign information:

* Campaign Name
* Channel
* Discount Type
* Discount Value
* Start Date
* End Date

### Channels

Contains sales channel information:

* E-Commerce
* App Mobile

### Stocks

Contains inventory information:

* Product ID
* Stock Quantity
* Stock Status

---

## Key Insights

* Shoes emerged as the highest-performing category in terms of revenue and inventory ROI.
* Germany contributed the highest customer activity among all countries.
* E-Commerce generated higher customer engagement compared to App Mobile.
* Inventory analysis identified low-stock products requiring replenishment.
* Social Media and Email campaigns showed strong promotional effectiveness.
* Revenue target achievement reached approximately 65% of the defined target.
